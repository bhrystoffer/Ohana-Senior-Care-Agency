Network-Coordinated

Lab work, at home — fast results through certified diagnostic partners.

Ohana coordinates access to at-home specimen collection and diagnostic services through CLIA-certified partner laboratories. No clinic trips required: a certified phlebotomist or collection specialist comes to the patient.

Tests Available

A wide range of diagnostic tests available through at-home collection, coordinated through our CLIA-certified partner network.

PCR & Respiratory Panels

COVID-19, influenza, RSV, and respiratory pathogen panels.

Blood Panels

CBC, CMP, HbA1c, lipid panel, thyroid function, and other routine blood work.

Wound Cultures

Wound swab culture and sensitivity for infection identification.

Urinalysis & UTI Testing

Urinalysis and urine cultures for UTI evaluation.

Specialty Diagnostics

Additional test panels available through our partner laboratory; confirm availability at time of request.

How It Works

From physician order to results delivery, Ohana manages the coordination so care teams can focus on the patient.

1

Physician Order Required

Most diagnostic tests require a physician order. Ohana confirms that an order is in place before coordinating collection.

2

Coordination Request

Submit a request through Ohana. We confirm test type, patient location, and scheduling availability.

3

At-Home Collection

A certified collection specialist from our partner laboratory visits the patient at home, SNF, or assisted living facility.

4

Results Delivery

Results are delivered to the ordering physician within the partner laboratory's standard turnaround, typically 24–48 hours.

Facilities Served

Mobile specimen collection is available for patients at:

  • Private residences
  • Skilled nursing facilities (SNFs)
  • Assisted living and memory care facilities
  • Hospice and palliative care settings

Our diagnostic network.

Ohana coordinates with Quantum Laboratory and other CLIA-certified diagnostic partners serving Southern California.

Ohana does not operate a laboratory and does not collect specimens directly. All specimen handling, testing, and results reporting are performed by CLIA-certified partner laboratories.
