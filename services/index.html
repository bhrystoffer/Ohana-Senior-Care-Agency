How Our Services Work

Two service models. Full transparency.

Ohana operates two types of services — those we run directly, and those we coordinate through our network of independently licensed providers. Every card in our service grid is labeled clearly.

Ohana-Direct
Operated directly by Ohana.

Ohana owns, operates, and staffs these services in-house. When you request an Ohana-Direct service, you are working with Ohana's own team — not a third-party provider.

Senior Placement Medical Transportation (NEMT)
Network-Coordinated
Coordinated through licensed partners.

Clinical services are provided by independent licensed agencies and practitioners within the Ohana network. Ohana manages coordination — intake, matching, communication, and follow-through — but clinical care is delivered by the partner.
