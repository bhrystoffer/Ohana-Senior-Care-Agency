The Process

A simple, human-led process.

Not an app. Not a chatbot. A real coordinator who picks up the phone, asks the right questions, and follows through from first call to ongoing care.

Submit a Request

A phone call or a quick form is all it takes. Tell us what the situation is, what's needed, and how urgent. We keep it simple: no lengthy intake questionnaires upfront.

Review & Assess

A coordinator reviews the request, asks the right clarifying questions, and identifies which service or combination of services fits the situation, including coverage and timeline.

Coordinate Access

We connect you to the right provider or partner agency, manage the introduction, share relevant background, and make sure nothing falls through the cracks at handoff.

Ongoing Support

We don't disappear after the first connection. We check in, follow through on open items, and stay available for as long as care is active, or until the next need arises.

Ready to start?

Most coordination requests receive a response within a few business hours. For urgent situations, call us directly.

Why Ohana

Infrastructure, not middlemen.

We don't sell leads. We don't collect referral fees. We build management infrastructure that keeps care moving for families, providers, and the partners in our network.

01

One Call. One Coordinator.

Families and providers never have to figure out who to call. One Ohana coordinator handles intake, routing, communication, and follow-through across every service.

02

Credential-Verified Network.

Every clinical partner in the Ohana network is independently licensed, credentialed, and verified before any referral is made. We verify every time, before the first case.

03

Active Coordination, Not Just Connection.

We don't hand off and disappear. Ohana stays involved, tracking open items, bridging communication gaps, and ensuring the right provider is actually showing up.

04

Compliant by Design.

Our coordination model is structured to keep clinical authority with licensed providers and administrative accountability with Ohana. No blurred lines, no compliance shortcuts.
