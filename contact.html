We take every inquiry seriously.

Care coordination starts with a conversation. Here's what happens after you reach out.

We receive your message

Every submission goes directly to a care coordinator — not a call center or automated queue. Someone reads it.

A coordinator will call or email to learn more about your situation and explain what coordination options are available.

Whether through Ohana directly or a vetted network partner, we identify the best fit — and stay involved through the handoff.
