About Ohana

Ohana means family.
That's how we built this.

Ohana Senior Care Agency is a healthcare management organization coordinating access to the full continuum of home-based and community care across Southern California. We exist to make the complicated simple and the overwhelming manageable.

Reach Out Our Services
Our Mission

Coordination is care, too.

For too many families, navigating senior care means making dozens of phone calls, deciphering insurance requirements, and hoping the right provider shows up. It shouldn't be that hard.

Ohana was built on a simple premise: when care coordination actually works: when someone takes ownership of the intake, the routing, the communication, and the follow-through, patients get better outcomes and families get their peace of mind back.

We are not a referral directory. We are not a search engine. We are an active coordination team that stays involved from first call through service delivery and remains your point of contact throughout.

"When care coordination actually works, families don't have to fight to get the help their loved one needs."

What we stand for

  • Making care access simple for families who are overwhelmed
  • Keeping clinical authority with licensed providers, always
  • Building partnerships that put patient outcomes first
  • Operating with the transparency and compliance that healthcare demands
Our Model

What Ohana does, and how we do it.

Ohana operates two types of services. Understanding this distinction is important for families choosing care, and for providers partnering with us.

Ohana-Direct

Services we operate in-house.

For our Ohana-Direct services, Ohana owns, staffs, and operates the service. When you call about Senior Placement or Medical Transportation, you are working with Ohana's own team from first contact to service completion.

  • Senior Placement Advisory
  • Non-Emergency Medical Transportation (NEMT)
Network-Coordinated

Services we coordinate through licensed partners.

For clinical and specialized services, Ohana manages intake, matching, communication, and follow-through, while clinical care is delivered by independent licensed providers within our credentialed partner network. We stay involved. We don't just hand off.

  • 11 coordinated clinical and support services
  • All partners independently licensed and verified
  • Ohana remains your point of contact throughout

How We Work

Four things we take seriously, every time.

Responsiveness

We pick up. We call back. We follow through. Being accessible and responsive is not a nice-to-have for us; it's the baseline for effective coordination.

Transparency

We tell you who does what, how services work, and what our role is in the process. No hidden handoffs. No ambiguity about who is responsible for what.

Active Coordination

We don't hand off and disappear. Ohana stays involved, bridging communication between families, providers, and facilities until the service is delivered and the outcome is confirmed.

Compliance

Our coordination model is structured to keep clinical authority with licensed providers, administrative accountability with Ohana, and compliance at the center of every relationship.

Our Values

What we hold ourselves to.

These aren't wall decorations. They're the standards we apply to every intake call, every coordination handoff, and every partner relationship we maintain.

01

Trust

Families let us into some of the most vulnerable moments of their lives. We treat that trust as the most important thing in the room, every time.

02

Clarity

Healthcare is full of complexity. Ohana's job is to absorb that complexity and give families and partners straightforward answers, not more confusion.

03

Compassion
